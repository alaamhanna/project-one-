The aim of this website

Welcome to our exciting website dedicated to the forthcoming inter-club competition at the Lebanese University! This unique event will bring together chess club, basketball club, programming club, book club, music club, and French language club. The competition unfolds with four initial groups, each comprising two dynamic clubs, vying for victory. As the rounds progress, clubs accumulate points, with the ultimate goal of securing the highest tally to claim the coveted championship title. Join us on this thrilling journey, as we showcase the diverse talents and passions of our university's vibrant club community, fostering friendly competition and camaraderie across multiple domains. Stay tuned for updates, highlights, and the crowning of the ultimate champion in this interdisciplinary clash of skills and creativity!

how the competition will go

Get ready for an exhilarating competition at the Lebanese University! The event kicks off with four initial groups, each consisting of two vibrant clubs set to go head-to-head. As the spirited clashes unfold, only one club from each group emerges victorious, resulting in four single-club groups. The excitement continues to escalate as the winners from two distinct groups face off, determined to secure a spot in the ultimate showdown. The climactic clash between these two champions will ultimately determine the crowning of the overall competition winner. With fierce battles, strategic maneuvers, and unwavering determination, the competition promises to showcase the talents and spirit of the participating clubs in a thrilling pursuit of victory. Stay tuned for the unfolding drama and the emergence of the ultimate champion in this dynamic inter-club competition!
